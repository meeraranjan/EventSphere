About

EventSphere is an interactive platform designed to help people discover, plan, and coordinate attendance at local events — all through a dynamic map-based interface.

Whether you're an event organizer promoting your next big gathering, an attendee looking for exciting things to do nearby, or an admin managing event data, EventSphere brings everything together in one cohesive experience.

With smart integrations like Google Maps and Google Calendar APIs, EventSphere makes it easy to visualize event locations, plan trips efficiently, and stay organized. The platform also incorporates cost and travel estimation tools, allowing users to budget their outings and coordinate with friends effortlessly.

EventSphere encourages social coordination and smart travel planning, transforming the way people engage with local events. By combining discovery, planning, and coordination into one place, it makes attending events not just easier — but more enjoyable and informed.
